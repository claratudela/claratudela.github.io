    View Site

  • Google World Cup Trends

    View Trends

    We analyzed search habits of two competing nations, then published illustrated stories - like brazilians searching for how to paint their country’s flag on their nails, or argentinians searching for prayers during the final. A team of data analysts, writers and designers worked around the clock to quickly create stories before, during and after the match. My role: campaign concepting, responsive site design, colour palette, story concepting, illustration creation, book design, case-study.
